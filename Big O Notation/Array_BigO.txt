Arrays (Ordered List)

When to use Arrays ? 
    - When you need order

Big O of Arrays 
    Searching - O(n)
    Accessing - O(1)
    Insertion - depends.. Inserting at the beginning has a Big O of O(n)
    Removal - depends... Removing at the beginning has a Big O of O(n)


Array methods Big O 
O(1) - .push() -Becasue you are just adding it to the end of the array (indexes stay the same)
O(1) - .pop()  - Becasue you are just removing from the end of the array (indexes stay the same)
O(n) - .forEach| .map | .reduce | .filter | .concat | .unshift | .splice
